Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled in the Classic Snake Game app, available as a Pi Network Ecosystem utility.

Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Data Collected: The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.
  • Local Storage: Your high score is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This data is not transmitted to any server and is only accessible on your device.
  • No Cookies or Tracking: The app does not use cookies or any third-party tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with the Pi Network SDK for authentication and payment features. Please refer to the Pi Network Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app developer through the Pi Network Ecosystem or the app's support channel.
